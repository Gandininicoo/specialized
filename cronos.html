S-Works SHIV TT

The fastest is now faster...
Specialized claims the outgoing SHIV TT was the most aerodynamically efficient UCI specified time trial bike available. The new SHIV looks to grow on that with a radical new frame shape designed to let its “Win Tunnel” optimized components cut through the air, mainly the Roval 321 Disc rear wheel.
Specialized claim it’s the fastest UCI certified disc rear wheel in the world; as a result, the SHIV is designed to work in conjunction with the wheel to provide unmatched aero efficiency. Consumers aren’t limited to using the Roval 321; however, with Specialized claiming most modern disc rear wheels provide similar aero benefits.
